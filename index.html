My Apology

My dearest [Her Name],

Sometimes my heart aches when I think about how I hurt you. It’s hard for me to say sorry face to face—I get lost for words, and I know I’m wrong. I never want to see pain in your beautiful eyes because of me, yet I did. I said things I never meant, and in a moment of frustration, I raised my hand. My guilt eats away at me.

The truth is, you are my world, and you deserved nothing but tenderness. Even when you tease me or playfully hit me, I always feel loved. But what I did wasn’t loving—it was out of line, and I’m so sorry.

I love you, and I never want to lose you or your trust. Please forgive me, my love.

Yours forever,
Nishant

My Vows To You

  • I vow to never, ever raise my hand in anger or frustration. My hands are meant to hold you gently and protect you.
  • I vow to speak to you with kindness, even when life tests us. I will listen deeply and never let my words wound you.
  • I vow to treasure every moment with you. To respect and adore you always. Loving you is my greatest gift, and I promise to never forget that.

When You Made Me Feel Loved

  • When you wrapped me in hugs after long days, I felt understood and cherished.
  • In your toughest pregnancy days, you still chose to stand by me and never let me feel alone, even when you could have sent me away for chores.
  • You defended me in front of your mother, even when she couldn’t see what you see in me. Your loyalty is my strength.
  • When you made me laugh, cooked my favorite food, or whispered sweet things before sleep—every moment was pure love.
  • When you forgave my mistakes and loved me anyway, you showed me what real love feels like.

When You’re Ready

My heart is open, waiting for you.
When you’re ready to forgive, I’ll be here—with open arms, so grateful, so in love.
